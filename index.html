What is The Nothing Coin?

The Nothing Coin is... or should we talk about what The Nothing Coin is not? Nothingcoin is neither centralized, nor decentralized. It does not utilize Proof of Work. It does not utilize Proof of Stake. It does not inflate; it does not deflate. It has no advantages, and does not have any disadvantages. It is the only coin in the nature of the world that does not have a correlation with the outside world.
